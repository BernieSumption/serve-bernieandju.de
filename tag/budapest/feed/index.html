Generally, when taking a bath, I prefer not to share the tub with innumerable rotund moustachioed Hungarian men. That said, walking barefoot through the snow is enough to make you relish the warm water regardless of the company.

Yes, it is as cold as it looks. The professionals take flip-flops, we just flapped our arms and screeched as we ran across the courtyard.
